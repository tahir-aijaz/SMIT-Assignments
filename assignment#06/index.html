Color Theory in Web Development:
Named colors provide readability and are perfect for quick prototyping. Colors like 'crimson', 'steelblue', and 'goldenrod' are self-explanatory.
HEX notation (#RRGGBB) offers precision with 16.7 million possible combinations. Each pair represents Red, Green, and Blue values from 00 to FF.
RGB values rgb(red, green, blue) use decimal numbers (0-255) and are intuitive for understanding color mixing. They also support transparency with RGBA.
Modern CSS also includes HSL (Hue, Saturation, Lightness) for more intuitive color adjustments.
